General Surgery

Our General Surgery Department provides comprehensive surgical care for a wide range of conditions. Our skilled surgeons are equipped to handle various procedures, from routine appendectomies to complex hernia repairs, ensuring patients receive the highest quality of care.

Urology Surgery Department

The Urology Surgery Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of urinary tract and male reproductive system disorders. Our expert urologists use advanced surgical techniques to address conditions such as kidney stones, prostate issues, and bladder problems, offering both minimally invasive and traditional surgical options.

Plastic Surgery Department

The Plastic Surgery Department at Beniwal Hospital is dedicated to enhancing and reconstructing physical appearance through advanced surgical techniques. Our experienced plastic surgeons provide a wide range of services, including cosmetic surgery, reconstructive procedures, and treatments for burns and trauma.

Medicine Department

Our Medicine Department focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases. With a team of experienced physicians, we offer comprehensive care for chronic conditions, acute illnesses, and preventive health services, ensuring our patients maintain optimal health and well-being.

Gynaecology & Obstetrics Department

The Gynaecology & Obstetrics Department provides specialized care for women's health issues. From routine gynecological exams and prenatal care to complex obstetric surgeries and fertility treatments, our team is committed to supporting women through all stages of life with compassionate and expert care.

Orthopaedic Surgery Department

The Orthopaedic Surgery Department at Beniwal Hospital specializes in diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal conditions. We offer advanced surgical and non-surgical treatments for bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles to help patients regain mobility and alleviate pain. Our expert team is committed to providing high-quality, personalized care for optimal outcomes.
